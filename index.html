MEAN

Dreadnought

September 2018

A web-based game build with MEAN technology stack. More...Less

In this game you can design ships, put weapons and decide the armor layout, and speed of your ships. Then build a fleet using a point buy system similar to miniature game.

Take your fleet to battle against the AI which will pick a random assortment of ships in three different game modes. You can move your ships and decide on which target to attack to attack or launch tropedo using a turn-base system. Combat algorithm takes into account for ship size, weapon class, armor thickness.

Python/Django

Food Truck Sim

July 2018

A web-based game written in Python using Django framework. More...Less

Open up foodtruck in major cities around the world. The game will pull real life weather data that can influence the demand of different type of food. Buy ingredients and combine them into different dishes. A algorithm will then determine how much of each dish is sold. Any leftover spoils so don't over produce!

Read up on graphical daily report and adjust your strategy. Then compete with other players through online scoreboard, become the best foodtruck in the world!

PokerFighter

August 2018

A console based game (console as in CLI, not PS4) written in C# as a summer hackathon project. More...Less

The idea is to build a RPG combat system similar to Puzzle Quest but using Poker instead of match-3 system.

Perform different type of attack with different effectiveness based on the cards you play. Your opponent fights back with various moves.
